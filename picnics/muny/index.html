The Muny Picnic

Near America's oldest outdoor theater · Four acclaimed eateries · Just bring yourselves

A One-of-a-Kind Culinary Experience in Forest Park

The Muny — America's oldest and largest outdoor musical theater — has been a cornerstone of St. Louis culture since 1919. It seats over 11,000 people under the stars every summer, and its surrounding grounds in Forest Park are among the most beloved in the city.

The Muny Picnic lets you experience this magical corner of Forest Park the way it was meant to be experienced: slowly, with great food, without a care in the world. Your basket comes packed with freshly-prepared greatest hits from four iconic St. Louis eateries — the kind of food that reminds you why this city has such a fierce and loyal food culture.

Everything you need for a perfect afternoon is in the basket. You just have to show up.

Sustainability & Community

TourCWE picnics are designed with environmental responsibility in mind. Your basket eliminates more than 80% of the packaging you'd generate at a comparable restaurant experience, and we don't use fossil fuels in our operations. For every picnic sold, $5 is donated to Forest Park Forever — the organization that stewards and maintains this incredible park.

The Setting: Near the Muny

The grounds surrounding the Muny are quiet, tree-shaded, and deeply St. Louis. While the theater itself only operates during the summer season, the park grounds are beautiful year-round and offer one of the most peaceful outdoor dining experiences in the region. It's the kind of afternoon that makes you fall in love with St. Louis all over again.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is this picnic available year-round?

We operate Wednesday–Saturday. Check the booking widget for current seasonal availability and specific dates.

Can I add more people to the picnic?

This picnic is portioned for two. For larger groups, contact us at info@tourcwe.com — we'd love to work something out.

What happens if it rains?

Light rain: bring a compact rain jacket or umbrella. The waterproof blanket keeps wet grass from soaking you from below, but it does not cover you from above. Severe weather: we'll work with you to reschedule or refund. Contact us at info@tourcwe.com if conditions are in question.

What's the cancellation policy?

Full refund up to 24 hours before your booking. Within 24 hours, the picnic is already being prepared and is non-refundable.
