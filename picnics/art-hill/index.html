Art Hill Picnic

St. Louis' most iconic vista · Four acclaimed eateries · Just bring yourselves

The Perfect Date in the Greatest City Park in America

Art Hill — the sweeping grand lawn leading up to the St. Louis Art Museum — is one of the most beautiful and iconic spots in all of Forest Park, and arguably in the entire city. In winter it's a sled run. In summer it's where St. Louisans come to watch movies under the stars. And any day of the week, it's where TourCWE sets you up for the most memorable afternoon you'll have in St. Louis.

Your Art Hill Picnic comes fully loaded: a basket curated from four acclaimed St. Louis eateries in the CWE and the Grove, set up with everything you need for a leisurely afternoon in the park. Appetizer, two entrees, dessert, a pint of ice cream that won't melt (we have a quadruple-insulated chiller for that), chilled water, a corkscrew, and a large waterproof blanket.

You don't have to plan a thing. Just show up, find your spot, and eat.

The Restaurants

Each basket features a rotating selection of freshly-prepared favorites from four iconic eateries in St. Louis' chic Central West End and bold Grove neighborhoods. The menu rotates to reflect the season and availability — every picnic is a surprise. What doesn't change: the quality, the quantity, and the fact that you'll be planning your next visit to these restaurants before you finish eating.

The Location: Art Hill, Forest Park

Forest Park covers 1,371 acres — larger than New York's Central Park — and is consistently ranked among the greatest urban parks in America. Art Hill sits at its heart, offering a sweeping view of the Grand Basin and the St. Louis Art Museum at its crown. On a clear day, it's one of the most beautiful spots in the Midwest. It's the perfect setting for an afternoon that goes nowhere fast.

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I receive my basket?

After booking, you'll receive pickup details via email. You'll collect your basket at a designated location and take it to your spot in Forest Park.

Can I choose what's in the basket?

The menu is curated and rotates based on seasonal availability and what's fresh from our restaurant partners. Think of it as a chef's selection — the surprise is part of the experience!

What's the cancellation policy?

Full refunds are available up to 24 hours before your booking time. Within 24 hours, cancellations are non-refundable as food has already been prepared.

Is this only for two people?

This picnic is designed and portioned for two. For larger groups, please contact us at info@tourcwe.com to discuss options.
