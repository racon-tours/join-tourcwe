TourCWE Brand Tenets

Internal reference for the TourCWE visual system. The pieces here are the canonical set — colors, typography, and logo lockups.

Palette

Blue#628395
Gold#d6ce93
Cream#efebce
Dark#1c1c1c
Sage#607466

Blue is primary. It replaces the old gold as the main accent color (wordmark, H1 emphasis, link hovers). Gold remains present as a warm secondary. Cream is the brand-light surface; Dark is the brand-dark surface.

Typography

Display face: Ubuntu Bold

TourCWE — See St. Louis rise and fall from the neighborhood built at its peak.
Ubuntu Bold 700. Used for the wordmark and every H1. <em> inside H1 = blue color accent (never italic).

Editorial face: Cormorant Garamond

Gilded Age wealth and the neighborhood St. Louis built at its peak.
Cormorant Garamond 500 italic. Used for H2–H4, pull quotes, and editorial flourishes.

Body face: Nunito

An award-winning St. Louis food + history walk, told from the Central West End.
Nunito 300/400/500/600/700. All body text, buttons, navigation, forms.
