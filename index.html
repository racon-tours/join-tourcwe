Meet Mike Nichols

Mike is a native historian, raconteur, and longtime CWE resident who's been quietly giving unauthorized neighborhood tours to friends and family for over a decade. A Paris food tour inspired him to make it official — and he hasn't stopped since.

"I want people to leave feeling like they better understand where St. Louis zigged when it should have zagged — and also to fall in love with the modern CWE."
